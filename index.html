Diego Carrillo

Biography

Diego Carrillo is a third-year computer science student at New Mexico State University, with an interest in software and web development. He serves as a senator for the college of Arts and Sciences in the Associated Students of New Mexico State University Senate. He is also the Vice President of the Sigma Chi Fraternity at NMSU. He is originally from Los Lunas, New Mexico.
